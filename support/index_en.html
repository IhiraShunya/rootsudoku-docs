Game Introduction

RootSudoku is a new puzzle game that combines the basic rules of Sudoku with the unique condition of perfect squares (roots).

In addition to the classic Sudoku rule of no duplicate numbers in rows, columns, and blocks, the following Root Rules are added for deeper logical thinking:

  • Red Frame: Concatenated numbers form a perfect square
  • Blue Frame: The sum of numbers is a perfect square
  • Green Frame: The product of numbers is a perfect square

Each board features a unique combination of frames to challenge your logic.
Highly recommended for those who want more than ordinary Sudoku.
Includes auto error check and a dedicated calculator for comfortable play!
